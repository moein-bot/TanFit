BMI Calculator

Calculate your Body Mass Index and understand your health status

What is BMI?

Body Mass Index (BMI) is a measure of body fat based on height and weight. It's a useful screening tool to categorize weight status and assess potential health risks.

Below 18.5 Underweight
18.5 - 24.9 Normal weight
25.0 - 29.9 Overweight
30.0 and above Obese

Our Story

Two friends united by passion for health and helping others

How TanFit Began

TanFit was born from a simple yet powerful vision: to make science-based nutrition accessible to everyone. What started as conversations between two passionate friends has evolved into a comprehensive platform dedicated to transforming lives through personalized nutrition.

Our Mission

We believe that everyone deserves access to personalized, science-based nutrition guidance. Our mission is to bridge the gap between complex nutritional science and practical, everyday meal planning. Through TanFit, we're making it possible for anyone to receive the kind of detailed, personalized nutrition advice that was once available only to professional athletes and those with access to expensive nutritionists.

Science-Based

Every recommendation is backed by peer-reviewed research in food science, sports science, anatomy, and biology.

Personalized

We understand that every body is unique. Our plans are tailored to your individual needs, goals, and lifestyle.

Accessible

Quality nutrition guidance shouldn't be a luxury. We're committed to making our services available to everyone.

Our Journey

The Beginning

Moein and Oweys met during their university studies, bonding over their shared passion for health, fitness, and helping others achieve their wellness goals.

The Idea

After witnessing friends and family struggle with generic diet advice, they realized the need for truly personalized, science-based nutrition guidance.

Research & Development

Months of research into nutrition science, AI algorithms, and user experience design culminated in the TanFit platform architecture.

TanFit Launch

Today, TanFit serves users with personalized diet plans based on comprehensive health assessments and cutting-edge nutritional science.

Our Vision

We envision a world where everyone has access to personalized nutrition guidance that's both scientifically accurate and practically applicable. Through continuous innovation and unwavering commitment to our users' success, we aim to become the global leader in personalized nutrition, helping millions of people achieve their health and fitness goals.
